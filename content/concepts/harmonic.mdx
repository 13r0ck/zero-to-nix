[Harmonic] is a non-official, opinionated installer for Nix created by [Determinate Systems][ds].
It currently supports both Linux and macOS. Harmonic differs from the [official Nix
installer][official] in several ways...

[harmonic]: https://github.com/DeterminateSystems/harmonic
[official]: https://nixos.org/download.html
