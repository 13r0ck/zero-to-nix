The **Nix store** is a storage system in your filesystem with its root at `/nix/store` by default. Nix stores several things in the Nix store:

- The products of [derivations](/concepts/derivation)

Paths in the Nix store have this structure:

<NixStorePath pkg="my-package-1.0" client:only />
