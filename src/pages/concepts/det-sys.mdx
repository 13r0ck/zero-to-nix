[Determinate Systems][ds]&mdash;sometimes shortened to **DetSys**&mdash;is a for-profit company committed to dramatically improving the experience around using [Nix] for people across our industry.

We've included an entry for Determinate Systems here to make it clear that this is a (hopefully clever) company name and *not* a special concept associated with Nix and its ecosystem.
