Flake **templates** are a feature of Nix that enables you to either initialize
a new project and... do other stuff.

You can initialize a flake template using the [`nix flake init`][init] command.

[init]: https://nixos.org/manual/nix/stable/command-ref/new-cli/nix3-flake-init.html
